## Introduction

Choosing plastic windows is a decision that will last for decades. One of the key factors affecting the quality, durability, and energy efficiency of windows is the choice of PVC profile. On the Ukrainian market, three German brands are the most popular: **Rehau**, **KBE**, and **Veka**.

In this article, we'll compare these profiles in detail across key parameters to help you make an informed choice for your home or apartment. The profile selection tips will be especially useful for residents of Kyiv and the Kyiv region, where climate conditions demand quality thermal insulation.

## General Brand Overview

### Rehau

**Rehau** is a German corporation with over 70 years of history. It's one of the world's largest PVC profile manufacturers, with its own factories in Germany, Poland, the Czech Republic, and other countries.

**Key advantages of Rehau:**
- High-quality PVC raw materials
- Excellent energy efficiency
- Wide range of profiles for different needs
- Durability (warranty up to 10 years)

**Popular product lines:**
- **Rehau Euro-Design 60** — basic, budget-friendly option
- **Rehau Brillant-Design 70** — optimal for most apartments
- **Rehau Geneo 86** — premium segment with the best thermal insulation

### KBE

**KBE (Kunststoffwerke Berger Elster)** is also a German manufacturer with over 40 years of experience, specializing in PVC profiles for windows and doors.

**Key advantages of KBE:**
- Optimal price-to-quality ratio
- Reliable construction
- Good thermal insulation
- Affordable price

**Popular product lines:**
- **KBE 70** — versatile profile for most applications
- **KBE 88** — energy-efficient option with 88 mm depth
- **KBE Expert** — professional series for complex projects

### Veka

**Veka** is one of the world's leading PVC profile manufacturers. Founded in Germany, the company has representatives in over 40 countries worldwide.

**Key advantages of Veka:**
- High structural strength
- Wide product range
- Modern design
- Excellent sound insulation

**Popular product lines:**
- **Veka SoftLine 60** — basic profile
- **Veka SoftLine 70** — improved thermal insulation
- **Veka SoftLine 82** — premium with maximum performance

## Technical Specifications: Comparison

Let's compare the key parameters of the most popular models from each brand:

| Parameter | Rehau Brillant-Design 70 | KBE 70 | Veka SoftLine 70 |
|----------|--------------------------|--------|-------------------|
| Profile depth | 70 mm | 70 mm | 70 mm |
| Number of chambers | 5 | 5 | 5 |
| Outer wall thickness | 3 mm | 3 mm | 3 mm |
| U-value of profile | 1.3 W/(m²·K) | 1.4 W/(m²·K) | 1.3 W/(m²·K) |
| Sound insulation | up to 45 dB | up to 43 dB | up to 46 dB |
| Wind load resistance | up to 6000 Pa | up to 5000 Pa | up to 6000 Pa |

### What do these parameters mean?

- **Profile depth** — the distance between the outer and inner walls. Greater depth means better thermal insulation.
- **Number of chambers** — cavities inside the profile that create an air gap. More chambers = better thermal and sound insulation.
- **Outer wall thickness** — affects strength and durability. Class A — 3 mm, Class B — 2.5 mm.
- **U-value** — a measure of thermal insulation. Lower values mean the profile retains heat better.

## Selection Tips

**1. Assess your room's needs**

For a mid-floor apartment, a 5-chamber 70 mm profile is sufficient. For a private house or a top/bottom floor apartment, consider a 6-7 chamber profile 80+ mm wide.

**2. Pay attention to the glass unit**

The profile is only half the success. A double-glazed unit with a low-emissivity coating provides up to 70% of the entire structure's thermal insulation.

**3. Check certifications**

Ask the seller for quality certificates on the profile. Original German profiles have corresponding markings and documentation.

**4. Calculate the total budget**

Don't forget to account for installation costs, windowsills, flashing, and additional options (mosquito nets, blinds, etc.).
