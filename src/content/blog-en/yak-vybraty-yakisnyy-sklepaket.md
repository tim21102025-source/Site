## Glass Unit Types and Their Features

**A glass unit** is a construction of two or more glass panes connected by a spacer frame and filled with air or gas. Choosing the right glass unit is key to comfort and energy savings, especially in Kyiv's climate.

**Single-chamber glass unit:**
- Consists of two glass panes
- Thickness: **4-6 mm** glass + **12-16 mm** air gap
- Thermal conductivity: **2.8 W/(m-K)**
- Used for unheated spaces or storefronts

**Double-chamber glass unit:**
- Consists of three glass panes
- Thickness: **4 mm** glass + **12 mm** air + **4 mm** glass + **12 mm** air + **4 mm** glass
- Thermal conductivity: **1.4 W/(m-K)**
- Most popular option for residential spaces

**Triple-chamber glass unit:**
- Consists of four glass panes
- Thickness: **4 mm** glass + **12 mm** air + **4 mm** glass + **12 mm** air + **4 mm** glass + **12 mm** air + **4 mm** glass
- Thermal conductivity: **0.8 W/(m-K)**
- Maximum thermal and sound insulation

> **Tip:** For most apartments, a double-chamber glass unit with energy-saving coating is optimal -- it provides good thermal insulation at a reasonable price.

## Energy-Saving Technologies

Modern glass units can have special coatings that significantly improve their thermal insulation properties.

**Soft (ITO) coating:**
- Applied by vacuum sputtering
- Thermal conductivity: **1.1 W/(m-K)**
- Heat savings: **30-40%**
- Lifespan: **10-15 years**
- Affordable price

**Hard (K) coating:**
- Applied by cathodic sputtering
- Thermal conductivity: **0.8 W/(m-K)**
- Heat savings: **40-50%**
- Lifespan: **20-25 years**
- Higher cost

**Energy-saving technology comparison:**

| Coating Type | Thermal Conductivity | Heat Savings | Lifespan | Price |
|-------------|---------------------|--------------|----------|-------|
| No coating | 1.4 W/(m-K) | - | - | Lowest |
| Soft (ITO) coating | 1.1 W/(m-K) | 30-40% | 10-15 years | Medium |
| Hard (K) coating | 0.8 W/(m-K) | 40-50% | 20-25 years | Higher |

**Additional energy-saving technologies:**
- **Argon filling** -- improves thermal insulation by 10-15%
- **Krypton filling** -- improves thermal insulation by 20-25% (expensive)
- **Vacuum glass units** -- maximum thermal insulation (exotic solution)

## Sound Insulation and Noise Reduction

Sound insulation is an important parameter for city apartments and houses near roads.

**Factors affecting sound insulation:**
- **Glass thickness** -- greater thickness = better sound insulation
- **Number of chambers** -- more chambers = better sound insulation
- **Different glass thicknesses** -- reduces resonance
- **Wider chamber** -- improves sound insulation

**Glass unit types for sound insulation:**

**Sound-insulating glass unit:**
- Glass of different thicknesses (e.g., 6 mm + 4 mm)
- Wider chamber (16-20 mm)
- May have laminated glass
- Noise reduction: **35-40 dB**

**Acoustic glass unit:**
- Special construction for maximum noise reduction
- Laminated glass with interlayer
- Noise reduction: **40-45 dB**
- Used near airports and railways

> **Fact:** A standard double-chamber glass unit reduces noise by **25-30 dB**, which can transform a noisy street into a quiet room.

## What to Look For When Choosing

Choosing the right glass unit requires attention to several key parameters:

**1. Glass type and thickness:**
- Standard glass: **4 mm**
- Reinforced glass: **6-8 mm**
- Tempered glass: **4-10 mm** (for safety)
- Laminated glass: **4-6 mm** (for sound insulation)

**2. Chamber width:**
- Minimum: **12 mm** (for standard glass units)
- Optimal: **16 mm** (for improved insulation)
- Maximum: **20 mm** (for acoustic glass units)

**3. Gas filling:**
- **Air** -- standard solution
- **Argon** -- improved thermal insulation (+10-15%)
- **Krypton** -- maximum insulation (+20-25%)

**4. Additional options:**
- **Energy-saving coating** -- essential for heated spaces
- **Solar control coating** -- for rooms with high sun exposure
- **[Sound-insulating coating](/service-details-glass)** -- for noisy Kyiv neighborhoods

**5. Quality certificates:**
- Check for conformity certificates
- Ask for manufacturer warranty
- Verify the manufacturer's reputation

> **Important:** Don't skimp on the glass unit -- a quality product will last longer and provide better comfort than a cheap alternative, especially during Kyiv's winters.

To make selection easier, here are our recommendations for different situations:

- **Double-chamber glass unit** with soft or hard coating

- Chamber width: 16 mm

**For a noisy neighborhood:**
- **Double-chamber acoustic glass unit**
- Laminated glass or different thickness panes
- Chamber width: 16-20 mm

- Argon or krypton filling

- **Triple-chamber glass unit** with dual energy-saving coating
- Maximum chamber width
- Krypton filling
- Additional window opening insulation
