## Introduction

Window replacement is an investment in the comfort and energy efficiency of your home. But before making a decision, it's important to understand what makes up the cost and how to calculate your budget. In this article, we'll take a detailed look at window replacement prices in Kyiv in 2026 and help you avoid unexpected expenses. These prices are relevant for residents of Kyiv and the Kyiv region.

## What's Included in the Window Cost?

The total cost of window replacement includes several components:

**1. PVC Profile** — the window frame that determines thermal and sound insulation.

**2. Glass Unit** — the glass part that provides light transmission and thermal insulation.

**3. Hardware** — opening mechanisms, locks, hinges.

**4. Installation Work** — removal of old windows and installation of new ones.

**5. Additional Elements** — windowsills, flashing, mosquito nets, blinds.

**6. Additional Services** — slope insulation, drip edge replacement, slope finishing.

## How to Save on Window Replacement?

**1. Order windows in advance**

Many companies offer discounts for off-season orders (spring, fall).

**2. Order multiple windows at once**

Bulk discounts can be 5-15% off the total cost.

**3. Choose the optimal profile**

You don't always need the premium segment. For a typical apartment with central heating, a 5-chamber 70 mm profile is sufficient.

**4. Don't save on installation**

Poor installation can lead to leaks, drafts, and voided warranties. This will cost much more than quality installation from the start.

**5. Skip unnecessary options**

Mosquito nets, blinds, and other additional elements can be purchased later if they're not urgently needed. ## Common Mistakes When Choosing Windows

**1. Skimping on the glass unit**

Many people choose the cheapest glass unit and then complain about cold rooms. An energy-saving glass unit costs 30-40% more but provides significantly better thermal insulation.

**2. Ignoring sound insulation**

For apartments near roads, sound insulation is a critical parameter. Choose a glass unit with different glass thicknesses or a special noise-reducing one.

**3. No flashing**

Flashing protects the wall from rainwater. Without it, moisture can penetrate the structure and cause mold.

**4. Wrong size**

Don't measure windows yourself. Incorrect measurements can lead to additional insulation work or even the need to remanufacture the window.
