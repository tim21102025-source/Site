## How to tell if a window drafts

**A draft near a window** is not just an unpleasant discomfort — it's a sign of serious insulation problems. It's important to identify and fix the cause in time to avoid high heating costs, especially in Kyiv with its cold winters.

**Signs that a window drafts:**

- **Feeling cold** near the window even with closed walls
- **Air movement** — you can feel it with your hand or see it with a lit candle
- **Condensation** on the glass or reveals
- **Freezing** of walls around the window opening
- **Increased heating costs** without obvious reasons
- **Mold** in window corners or on reveals

> **Test:** Run a lit candle or match along the window frame. If the flame wavers — this is a clear sign of a draft.

## Main causes of drafts

Window drafts can have various causes, and correct diagnosis is key to an effective solution.

**1. Worn or damaged seals:**
- Seals lose elasticity over time
- Can be damaged during use
- Incorrect seal type selection

**2. Improper hardware adjustment:**
- Insufficient sash clamping against the frame
- Sash sagging due to load
- Wear of mechanical parts

**3. Installation problems:**
- Inaccurate window frame installation
- Missing or damaged mounting foam
- Violation of installation technology

**4. Window opening problems:**
- Cracks in the wall around the window
- Insufficient reveal insulation
- Thermal bridges due to structural features

**5. Profile deformation:**
- Temperature deformation
- Mechanical damage
- Low material quality

## Step-by-step diagnosis guide

### Step 1: Visual inspection

Carefully inspect the window from outside and inside. Pay attention to:
- Visible cracks or damage
- Seal condition (elasticity and integrity)
- Presence and condition of mounting foam
- Window frame levelness

### Step 2: Draft test

Use one of these methods:
- **Candle or match:** Run along the frame and reveals
- **Tissue:** Run a thin tissue along the seams
- **Hand:** Run your hand along the frame, feeling for air movement

### Step 3: Adjustment check

Check how the hardware works:
- Does the sash open and close easily
- Is there sufficient clamping when closed
- Are there any plays in the mechanisms

### Step 4: Temperature measurement

Use a thermometer or thermal camera:
- Measure the temperature on the frame and adjacent wall
- Compare readings with the room temperature
- A difference of more than 5-7°C indicates a problem

> **Tip:** Write down the diagnosis results — this will help the specialist identify the problem faster.

## Solving the problem

After identifying the cause of the draft, choose the appropriate solution:

**Solution 1: Seal replacement**
- Remove the old seal
- Clean the groove of dirt and residue
- Install a new seal of the appropriate type
- Check the tightness

**Solution 2: [Hardware adjustment](/service-details-repair)**
- Adjust the clamping with a screwdriver or wrench
- Set the optimal mode for the season
- Check the operation of all mechanisms

**Solution 3: Joint sealing**
- Remove old mounting foam
- Fill gaps with new foam or sealant
- Smooth and paint the surface

**Solution 4: Reveal insulation**
- Install insulation on the reveals
- Plaster and paint
- Ensure tight joints

**Solution 5: Contact a specialist**
- If the problem can't be solved independently
- For complex structural damage
- For comprehensive work

> **Important:** Don't attempt to solve complex problems on your own — incorrect actions can worsen the situation and lead to additional costs.

## Prevention and maintenance

To avoid draft problems in the future, follow these simple rules:

**Regular maintenance:**
- **Adjust hardware** twice a year (before winter and summer)
- **Lubricate mechanisms** with specialized lubricant every 6 months
- **Clean seals** of dirt and dust

**Seasonal preparation:**
- **In winter:** Check tightness before the heating season
- **In summer:** Check seal and hardware condition
- **In autumn:** Perform additional sealing if needed

**Professional maintenance:**
- Call a professional for comprehensive diagnosis every 2-3 years
- Replace worn parts in a timely manner
- Follow manufacturer recommendations

> **Prevention:** Regular window maintenance in Kyiv costs significantly less than repair or replacement. Invest in prevention to avoid major expenses.
